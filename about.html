About Me

Having a passion for Full Stack web development with a particular interest in the Backend and Database Management, I am ready to begin a new venture in the IT world. Having just completed an intensive 24-week coding bootcamp, I now have the skills necessary to have a positive impact in the world of web development.

After receiving a Bachelor's of Music in Clarinet Performance, I moved to Durham, North Carolina, and established a successful clarinet and saxophone studio, currently teaching 40-50 middle and high school students each week. Several students have moved onto advanced studies at some of the country's finest music schools.

Having established a successful private studio and performing career in central North Carolina, I am ready for an exciting new change!
